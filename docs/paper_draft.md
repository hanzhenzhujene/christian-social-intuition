- **Methods**

- **Results**

  - `J1→J2` revision is rare
- **Discussion**
  - supports mechanism separation
  - argues against naive reading of bundled outputs
  - favors stage dissociation plus partial rhetorical uptake over a strong uniquely Christian semantic advantage
