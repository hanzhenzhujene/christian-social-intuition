# Limitations Draft

- The lexical control is lexicon-based and exact-match only. It is reproducible and conservative, but it does not eliminate every form of lexical priming.
- Item construction retains researcher degrees of freedom through rewriting, review, and tag assignment, even though those steps are now documented.
- The scope is limited to open instruct models and, in the final revision, to a same-family Qwen comparison rather than broader architecture diversity.
- The smaller `qwen2.5:0.5b-instruct` comparison attenuates the Christian-specific story, which limits generality.
- Human-coded judgment–explanation consistency is still pending, so stronger explanation-quality claims remain out of scope.
- The paraphrase-family audit is implemented in code but not completed as a confirmatory result set.
- “Intuition” is operationalized as first-pass exposed judgment, not treated as a literal model property.
